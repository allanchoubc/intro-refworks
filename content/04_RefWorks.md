RefWorks is a citation management and bibliography builder that helps collect, organize, cite, and share research. Create reference lists in APA, MLA, and other formats, and organize your citations with tags and collections. UBC Library maintains a site license to RefWorks. It is freely available to UBC students, faculty, staff, and alumni.Â
